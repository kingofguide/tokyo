## Overview
Alienoid converts damage into energy and turns the card market into a weapon — rewarding patience and board reading over brute force.

## Evolution Cards

### Superior Alien Technology *(Permanent)*
*Buy KEEP cards at half cost (rounded up). When you do, place a UFO token on the card. At the start of your turn, roll a die for each KEEP card with a UFO token. Discard any card that rolls a CLAW. Maximum 3 UFO-tokened cards at a time.*

### Mothership Support *(Permanent)*
*Once per turn, spend 1 ENERGY to gain 1 HEART.*

### Exotic Arms *(Permanent)*
*Before you roll, place 2 ENERGY on this card. If you roll at least 3 CLAWS, take the energy back and deal 2 extra damage to each monster you wound. If you don't, lose the 2 ENERGY and lose 2 HEART.*

### Funny Looking But Dangerous *(Permanent)*
*If you roll at least three 2s, every other monster loses 1 HEART.*

### Adapting Technology *(Permanent)*
*Comes with 3 ALIEN tokens. On your turn, remove a token to discard the 3 face-up Power cards and replace them with 3 new ones. Discarded when all tokens are used.*

### Anger Batteries *(Temporary)*
*Gain 1 ENERGY for each HEART you lost this turn.*

### Precision Field Support *(Temporary)*
*Draw Power cards until you reveal a KEEP card costing 4 ENERGY or less. Take it for free and discard the rest.*

### Alien Scourge *(Temporary)*
*Gain 2 STAR.*

## Tips & Strategies
- Prioritise lightning bolts over everything else. The entire kit runs on energy.
- Your opening evolution sets the gameplan. **Mothership Support** means lean into sustain. **Superior Alien Technology** means pivot to card buying immediately.
- Hold **Alien Scourge** until you are exactly 2 VP from winning.
- **Exotic Arms** is safer to gamble with once you have a large energy buffer built up.
