## Overview
The King owns Tokyo, stacks VP from multiple sources simultaneously, and has tools to reclaim the throne the moment it's lost — rewarding positional play over raw aggression.

## Evolution Cards

### Twas Beauty Killed the Beast *(Permanent)*
*While in Tokyo, gain 1 STAR at the end of every monster's turn including yours. Discard this card and lose all your STAR if you leave Tokyo.*

### I Am the King! *(Permanent)*
*Gain 1 extra STAR when you take control of Tokyo or start your turn there.*

### Alpha Male *(Permanent)*
*If you wound at least one monster with CLAWS, gain 1 STAR.*

### Chest Thumping *(Permanent)*
*You can force monsters you wound with CLAWS to yield Tokyo.*

### Giant Banana *(Temporary)*
*Gain 2 HEART.*

### Jungle Frenzy *(Temporary)*
*Play at the end of your movement phase, if you wounded the Tokyo controller with CLAWS but didn't take control. Take an extra turn.*

### Simian Scamper *(Temporary)*
*If you yield Tokyo, do not lose HEART. You can't lose HEART this turn.*

### Monkey Rush *(Temporary)*
*Play when the monster controlling Tokyo leaves or is eliminated. Take control of Tokyo.*

## Tips & Strategies
- Get into Tokyo fast and establish **I Am the King!** first. Low risk and it pays off every single turn from that point.
- Hold **Monkey Rush** in hand at all times until Tokyo needs to be reclaimed urgently.
- With **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast** and **I Am the King!** both active, VP is generating passively every round. Play defensively and let the clock win.
- **Jungle Frenzy** is a strong closer. A bonus turn when near 20 VP can end the game immediately.
