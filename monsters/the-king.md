## Overview
A giant gorilla with one obsession: owning Tokyo. Every evolution card either rewards being in Tokyo, punishes opponents for trying to remove control, or reclaims the throne the moment it's lost. Energy and cards are less important as the Tokyo throne is the goal.

A strong pick for players who enjoy positional play and VP accumulation over raw aggression.

## Playstyle
The King is a **Defender** and **Scorer**. The evolutions make the gameplan explicit: **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast** generates VP at the end of every monster's turn while in Tokyo, **I Am the King!** doubles the standard Tokyo VP rewards, and **Monkey Rush** reclaims Tokyo instantly the moment someone else leaves. Every card in the kit exists to hold Tokyo or punish those who challenge it.

The core loop is: **get into Tokyo, stack VP from multiple sources simultaneously, never let anyone take it**.

## Evolution Cards
The King's eight evolution cards are almost entirely focused on Tokyo control and VP generation. Getting even two or three active simultaneously creates a VP engine that's very hard to race against.

### Twas Beauty Killed the Beast *(Permanent)*
*While in Tokyo, gain 1 STAR at the end of every monster's turn including yours. Discard this card and lose all your STAR if you leave Tokyo.*

The most powerful and most dangerous evolution in the kit. This generates VP compounding fast each round. The catch is brutal: leaving Tokyo costs every star accumulated on this card. Never play it without enough health to absorb pressure and either Simian Scamper or Chest Thumping ready as insurance.

### I Am the King! *(Permanent)*
*Gain 1 extra STAR when you take control of Tokyo or start your turn there.*

A straightforward VP amplifier. Taking Tokyo now gives 2 VP instead of 1, and starting a turn there gives 2 VP instead of 1. Low risk, permanent benefit. Get this out early and it pays off every single turn.

### Alpha Male *(Permanent)*
*If you wound at least one monster with CLAWS, gain 1 STAR.*

Turns every claw turn into a VP gain regardless of Tokyo position. Opponents can't ignore The King just because control of Tokyo was yielded. Stack with I Am the King! for VP generation from multiple sources every turn.

### Chest Thumping *(Permanent)*
*You can force monsters you wound with CLAWS to yield Tokyo.*

Control made absolute. Instead of waiting for an opponent to choose to yield, the decision is yours. Hit a Tokyo occupant hard enough and eject them immediately. Pairs perfectly with Twas Beauty Killed the Beast since getting back into Tokyo on demand protects the VP stack.

### Giant Banana *(Temporary)*
*Gain 2 HEART.*

Simple sustain. Use it when health drops low enough that staying in Tokyo becomes risky. Two health can be the difference between riding out one more round of attacks and being forced to yield and losing everything on Twas Beauty Killed the Beast.

### Jungle Frenzy *(Temporary)*
*Play at the end of your movement phase, if you wounded the Tokyo controller with CLAWS but didn't take control. Take an extra turn.*

A precision tempo card. If the Tokyo occupant was wounded but didn't yield, a full bonus turn triggers immediately. Use it to keep pressing, roll for VP, or set up the killing blow.

### Simian Scamper *(Temporary)*
*If you yield Tokyo, do not lose HEART. You can't lose HEART this turn.*

The safety net for Twas Beauty Killed the Beast. If forced to yield, at least walking away at full health is guaranteed. Also useful as a general defensive card when opponents are stacking claw damage and survival is the priority.

### Monkey Rush *(Temporary)*
*Play when the monster controlling Tokyo leaves or is eliminated. Take control of Tokyo.*

An instant Tokyo grab triggered by someone else's misfortune. Hold this in hand and one monster's bad luck is all it takes to reclaim the throne. Best played in response to a high-health monster being forced out before anyone else can react.

## Tips & Strategies

### Early Game
- Get into Tokyo fast and establish **I Am the King!** first. Low risk and it pays off every single turn from that point.
- Hold **Monkey Rush** in hand at all times until Tokyo needs to be reclaimed urgently.

### Late Game
- With **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast** and **I Am the King!** both active, VP is generating passively every round. Play defensively and let the clock win.
- **Jungle Frenzy** is a strong closer. A bonus turn when near 20 VP can end the game immediately.

### Evolution Combos
- **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast + I Am the King! + Alpha Male** — VP from three sources every round. Opponents must respond immediately or lose the VP race entirely.
- **Chest Thumping + Monkey Rush** — force anyone out of Tokyo with claws then instantly reclaim it. Near-permanent Tokyo control.
- **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast + Simian Scamper** — accumulate VP safely in Tokyo with Simian Scamper as the escape hatch that costs no health on the way out.

## Common Mistakes
- Activating **Twas Beauty Killed the Beast** too early without health insurance, losing all accumulated stars to a forced yield is catastrophic
- Holding **Monkey Rush** too long and letting someone else grab Tokyo when it was needed
- Ignoring **Alpha Male**, VP from claw turns outside Tokyo adds up faster than expected
- Staying in Tokyo at 3-4 health hoping to survive one more round, know when to yield before being forced out
- Treating **Chest Thumping** as reactive rather than proactive, use it to eject threats before they become dangerous
